Welcome to Burgas!

Burgas, another gem on Bulgaria’s Black Sea coast, is famous for its relaxed seaside atmosphere and ecological richness. The city offers easy access to scenic spots like the protected wetlands of Lake Atanasovsko, a haven for birdwatchers. Its Sea Garden, a coastal park with beautiful pathways and ocean views, is a favorite for locals and tourists alike. Burgas also has a thriving arts scene, with numerous summer festivals and an annual sand sculpture festival that transforms the beach. Known for its laid-back vibe, Burgas is a wonderful place to enjoy nature, culture, and the coast.
